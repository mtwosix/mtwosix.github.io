01

The Studio

Imagination is infrastructure — something a city runs on, something you can plan, build, and maintain.

M26 is a design studio at CEPT for futures thinking and systems design. Over eighteen weeks the cohort files its thinking as it happens — a note, a sketch, a film, a reading, a model — and the archive assembles itself around them, in public, in real time.

The studio does not wait for a final review to make the work visible. The record is the pedagogy: the semester accumulates as the field of points behind this page — every point a real submission, every thread of colour one student's line through time.

01·A

How it works

1 — FILE
A student files work through a Google Form: who, what kind, an attachment or a note. Any hour, any day — the record keeps the timestamp.
2 — COMMIT
A script moves each entry from the form's sheet into data/submissions.csv in the studio's repository — one row, one work, one point.
3 — MIRROR
This site reads that file and nothing else. What you see is a strict 1:1 mirror of the record — nothing invented, nothing curated away.
